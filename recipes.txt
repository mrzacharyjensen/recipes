# Margaritas

## Classic Margarita
1/2 Salt Rim
30ml Tequila
15ml Triple Sec
60ml Sour Mix
10ml Agave Syrup (optional)
Dried Lemon Garnish

## Fruit Margarita

30ml Tequila

30ml Fruit Syrup
